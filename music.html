Music That Inspires Me

Music has always played a big part in my life. Whether I’m relaxing after work, getting motivated during the day, or simply enjoying a moment of peace, there’s always a song that fits the mood. I enjoy a wide range of music, from R&B and gospel to hip-hop and soul. Artists like Rihanna, SZA, Kirk Franklin, and Keisha Cole are some of my favorites. Their music lifts me up, energizes me, and reminds me to stay grounded.

My Favorite Genres

  • R&B – smooth, soulful, and emotional
  • Gospel – spiritual and uplifting
  • Hip-Hop – rhythmic and expressive
  • Usher – relaxing and deep
